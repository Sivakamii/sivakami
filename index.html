About Me

I'm Sivakami, a Senior UI/UX Designer based in Dubai with 7+ years of experience designing and delivering digital experiences across web applications, enterprise systems, and real world environments.

I specialize in combining UX thinking, research, wireframing, and prototyping with frontend implementation (HTML, CSS, JavaScript). I work closely with developers beyond design handoff, ensuring that every solution is practical, scalable, and executed with precision.

My work extends beyond traditional interfaces. I've designed and deployed digital signage systems for enterprise clients, built complete brand identities and websites from scratch, and contributed to accessible healthcare experiences. This breadth allows me to approach problems with both creativity and system level thinking.
